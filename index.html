Santa Barbara Courthouse

Ava and Kevin's wedding day is set for Tuesday, June 16, 2026 at 3:30 PM at the Santa Barbara Courthouse.

The current courthouse was completed in 1929 after the 1925 earthquake, with architect William Mooser III shaping a Spanish Colonial Revival landmark. Its clock tower, Sunken Gardens, arches, tilework, and Mural Room make the building one of Santa Barbara's most beloved civic spaces.
